The Cheetah

Scientific name:Acinonyx Jubatus

Life Span:Up to 14years

Speed: 80-130km/hr

Kingdom:Animalia

With the Cheetah's unique body structure;flexible spine, semi-retactable claws, long legs and tail allows it to achieve an unbelievable speed that is above 100km/hr. The cheetah's body is narrow and lightweight with long slender limbs. Specialized muscles allow for a greater swing to the limbs increasing acceleration.
